Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How long does it take to build a website?

Standard websites typically take 2-4 weeks from start to finish. For simpler projects like landing pages, we offer an express 48-hour service. More complex projects with custom functionality may take 6-8 weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a specific timeline based on your project requirements.

Do you provide domain registration and hosting?

Yes, we provide domain registration and hosting services through our trusted partners like Namecheap, Hostinger, and SiteGround. We can handle the entire setup process for you, or work with your existing providers. Our team will recommend the best hosting solution based on your website's needs.

What types of IT solutions do you offer?

Our IT solutions include custom software development, business process automation, IT infrastructure setup, cloud solutions, digital transformation consulting, and database management. We tailor our services to meet your specific business requirements, helping you streamline operations and improve efficiency.

How do your pricing plans work?

We offer both project-based pricing and monthly plans. Project-based pricing provides a one-time fee for website development, branding, or digital marketing campaigns. Monthly plans are available for ongoing services like maintenance, SEO, or content management. All our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees, and we provide detailed proposals before starting any work.
