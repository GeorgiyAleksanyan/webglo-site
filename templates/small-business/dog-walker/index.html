Founded in 2020, Dog Walker Pro was created by passionate dog lovers who believe every pet deserves care, exercise, and joy. Our mission is to make every walk a highlight of your dog's day.

Our team is background-checked, insured, and trained in pet safety. We treat every dog as our own and strive to build lasting relationships with pets and owners alike.
