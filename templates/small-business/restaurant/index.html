Our Culinary Philosophy

At The Gilded Spoon, we believe dining is more than just food; it's an experience. Born from a desire to blend classic techniques with modern innovation, our kitchen is a playground of flavor, texture, and artistry.

We are committed to using the finest locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients to craft dishes that are both familiar and surprising. Every plate that leaves our kitchen is a testament to our passion for quality and our dedication to our guests.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Antoine Dubois

With over 20 years of experience in Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe, Chef Dubois brings a wealth of knowledge and a touch of French elegance to The Gilded Spoon. His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredients and let their quality shine.

"For me, cooking is not a job, it's a love affair. Every dish is a story, and I am thrilled to share my stories with you."
