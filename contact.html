Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple websites can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects may take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance plans that include security updates, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support. We're here to help your website continue to perform at its best.

What's included in the free consultation?

During our free consultation, we'll discuss your goals, review your current online presence, provide strategic recommendations, and create a custom proposal with pricing and timeline for your project.

Can you work with my existing website?

Absolutely! We can redesign, optimize, or add new features to your existing website. We'll evaluate what can be improved and what should be rebuilt from scratch for the best results.
